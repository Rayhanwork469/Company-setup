Your company name must be unique and not infringe upon reserved or trademarked names.  
- Submit 3‚Äì5 name options via the [Sijilat portal](https://www.moic.gov.bh).
- Names can be in English or Arabic.
- Use of ‚ÄúBahrain‚Äù or ‚ÄúGulf‚Äù may require special permission.

### 5.3 Commercial Registration (CR) Explained 

CR is your golden ticket‚Äîit‚Äôs the official license to operate.  
- Submit required info (owner details, passport, address, activity).
- Pay CR fees (BHD 100‚Äì350, depending on activity).
- Approval in as little as 3‚Äì7 working days for standard sectors.

**Full CR how-to:** [Commercial Registration in Bahrain](https://keylinkbh.com/commercial-registration-in-bahrain/)

### 5.4 Memorandum of Association & Articles

Banks in Bahrain are strict on compliance (AML/CFT), so you‚Äôll need:

- Expat founders/employees require work visas, processed via LMRA (Labor Market Regulatory Authority).

- Many sectors in Bahrain allow up to 100% foreign ownership‚Äîcompare with UAE or Saudi.
